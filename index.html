Concordion

A test runner that creates attractive living documentation

FLEXIBLE

Unlike other tools, you can write your specifications in normal language and use features such as hyperlinks and images in your documentation.

SIMPLE

Built with a purposely small command set, you can learn Concordion quickly. It's also simple to integrate with your existing development tools.

EXTENSIBLE

The powerful extension mechanism allows you to add functionality and customise existing features. Many pre-packaged extensions are available.
