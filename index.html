Concordion

Test Automation + Delightful Documentation


Get Started

Concordion is great for Specification by Example (SbE) and Behaviour Driven Development (BDD)

FLEXIBLE

Unlike other tools, you can write your specifications in normal language and use features such as hyperlinks and images in your documentation.

SIMPLE

Built with a purposely small command set, you can learn Concordion quickly. It's also simple to integrate with your existing development tools.

EXTENSIBLE

The powerful extension mechanism allows you to add functionality and customise existing features. Many pre-packaged extensions are available.

Attractive output

Tables
Tables

When you want to show several examples of a behaviour, tables provide a concise alternative to repeating the same sentence structure over and over again.

You can also verify a list of results against a table.

Indexes
Indexes

You can link to other specifications to run them, with the output colour-coded to show success, failure or ignored status.

You can even nest the pages to form a hierarchical index, with aggregated results.

Breadcrumbs
Breadcrumbs

To make it easier to navigate around the results, and to remove the headache of having to maintain upward links manually, breadcrumbs are automatically added to output.

Screenshots
Screenshots

The screenshot extension adds screenshots to steps in your specification output.

The storyboard extension adds a storyboard with screenshots and/or text cards to the bottom of your specification output.
